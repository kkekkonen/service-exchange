About Us

We offer a service exchange platform.

Nowadays people are becoming increasingly busy with their ordinary life due to several reasons, such as increased requirements from work life or school. This can lead to a situation where small tasks build up and cause stress when there are no helping hands. We provide a service exchange platform where people can easily exchange small services with other people. This platform consists of two parties; the people who need some service and the people who can provide their services. Using our platform these two parties can find each other and find a suitable deal for the service that satisfies both.
